# 对我影响最大的三条

- 用长期关系和长期项目构建复利
- 用代码、媒体和资本作为杠杆
- 把注意力从“忙碌”转向“可复用价值”

## 我的执行方式

我把“杠杆”翻译成可执行动作：每周沉淀一份可复用模板，而不是只完成一次性任务。

## 为什么推荐

它不是教你短期爆发，而是帮助你建立更稳的长期策略。

<!-- EN -->

# Three ideas that changed my behavior

- Build compounding through long-term projects and relationships
- Use code, media, and capital as leverage
- Optimize for reusable value instead of visible busyness

## How I execute this

I translate leverage into one repeatable action: ship one reusable template every week.

## Why I recommend it

It is less about quick wins and more about long-term strategic stability.
